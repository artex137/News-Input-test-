Santa Claus Eats Satanists on Halloween
By The Search 433
Published: October 31, 2025 at 11:11PM EDT
A witness captures Santa Claus devouring cloaked satanists during a bizarre Halloween incident in 2025. (Handout)

A shocking Halloween event unfolded in the suburbs of Burnaby, B.C., where eyewitnesses claim Santa Claus appeared from the sky and consumed a group of cloaked Satanists mid-ritual. The incident, which occurred just after 10 p.m., has left the community stunned and investigators scrambling for answers.

According to multiple reports, the Satanist group had gathered in a cul-de-sac to conduct a ceremonial chant when Santa allegedly descended via sleigh, surrounded by red mist and sleigh bells. He reportedly shouted, "You've all been very naughty!" before launching into what onlookers described as “carnivorous holiday fury.”

Authorities have yet to determine whether the attacker was a costumed individual or an unknown entity. However, the scene included scattered wrapping paper, peppermint shrapnel, and half-consumed ritual symbols.

One traumatized child received a gift-wrapped PS5 during the chaos and said, “He smiled at me… then bit that guy’s arm off.” The RCMP urges calm while investigations continue. North Pole officials have declined to comment.
